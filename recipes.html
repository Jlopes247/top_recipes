Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti or linguine
  • 6 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti or linguine and cook according to the package directions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the pasta and return it to the pot.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, cook the pancetta or bacon in a large skillet over medium heat until crisp and browned. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the pancetta to a paper towel-lined plate.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
  4. Add the cooked pancetta to the pot with the pasta and toss to combine. Then, pour the egg mixture over the pasta and toss until the pasta is coated in the egg mixture.
  5. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta cooking water, a tablespoon at a time, until the desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve the pasta carbonara hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.
